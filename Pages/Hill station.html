Raigad Fort

Raigad Fort is located on the shores of the Arabian Sea. The short, rocky formations make up the Raigad Fort unlike the Marina Beach in Chennai that is primarily sandy. It is a tourist attraction throughout the year and is also a destination for shooting films.
